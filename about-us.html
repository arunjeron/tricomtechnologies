About Us

Tricom Technologies, the company promoted by highly qualified technocrats having over 25 years of experience in designing and manufacturing various kinds of telecom and allied products for Indian telecom industry. This company was founded in June 1995 with the primary objective of offering solutions to the Department of Telecommunications for various applications.

Tricom Technologies is providing solutions in the areas of Telecommunications, Data communications, Computer Networking, Microelectronics, High Speed Digital Design Billing and Customer Care Telecom network monitoring Industrial Communication, CRM products and Signal processing.

This company has received an ISO 9001:2015 award and aims at providing solutions that addresses to all the needs of the customer and enhances the value of the customerâ€™s offering in the market place.

Indigenous development of technology, customization of product for specific applications, state of the art manufacturing and assured after sales support are the goals of the company.

Most of the products are approved by Telecom Engineering Centre, Department of Telecommunications. The Company is registered with Small Scale Industries (SSI), Directorate of Industries and Commerce and Registered with The National Small Industries Corporation Limited (NSIC) under single point registration scheme.
